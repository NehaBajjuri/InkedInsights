5 Must-Visit Places in India to Experience Autumn

Ankush 21-Feb-2023 2

The autumn season in India is a magical time of the year and is one of the most beautiful seasons in India. The weather is pleasant, the skies are clear, and the landscapes are ablaze with warm hues of red, orange, and yellow. For those who l ove to travel, the autumn season is the perfect time to explore new places and experience the beauty of nature.

Shimla: one of the best places to explore in the Autumn Season Shimla offers ample outdoor attractions to appeal to all types of visitors. Families may take advantage of the well-known Toy Train Ride, and nature enthusiasts can marvel at the stunning vistas that make up Summer Hill and Scandal Point. The Military Museum offers tours for history buffs, while the Shimla Ridge is a blast for adventurers. No matter what kind of traveller you consider yourself to be, Shimla's striking architecture and picturesque surroundings won't let you down. Shimla Offers a wide range of recreational locations, so you'll never get tired of having fun. Shimla Offers

McLeod Ganj, Himachal Pradesh: McLeod Ganj is a small, peaceful town in the mountains of Himachal Pradesh that is home to the Dalai Lama and the center of Tibetan culture in India. During the autumn season, the town is surrounded by golden hues as the leaves change color, and the cool mountain air provides a welcome respite from the heat of the plains. Visitors can hike through the surrounding hills, visit the many Tibetan monasteries and temples, and indulge in delicious local cuisine.

Leh-Ladakh: This beautiful destination located in the northernmost part of India is known for its stunning landscapes and adventure opportunities. During the autumn season, the skies are clear and the weather is perfect for outdoor activities such as trekking, camping, and rafting. Leh-Ladakh is also famous for its rich cultural heritage and unique landscapes that range from barren deserts to snow-capped mountains. Know more about places to visit in Leh-Ladakh.

Coorg: Coorg, commonly referred to as Kodagu, is a stunning highland town situated in the state of Karnataka. This destination is known for its stunning coffee plantations, rolling hills, and rich wildlife. During the autumn season, the weather is perfect for outdoor activities such as trekking, camping, and bird-watching. Coorq is also famous for its unique cuisine and rich cultural heritage, making it a must-visit destination for those looking to experience the essence of South India.
